Moline

John Deere

Moline is home to John Deere's many different sites and experiences:

  • The John Deere Pavillion is a major tourist site in the Quad Cities where people of all ages can enjoy and learn about the the new technologies of today and the future.
  • The John Deere Headquaters is an architectural feat. Although, the display has been closed since the pandemic, there is a path that runs around the property that many enjoy going on walk and runs on. The pond in front of the building is home to many fish, swans, and geese.
  • The John Deere store has John Deere souvenir for all: souvenirs big and small, and for all interests.

The Mississippi River

There are many ways to appreciate the Mighty Mississippi River:

  1. The Celebration River Cruises has many curise options on the river, including an 800-passenger luxury river cruise called the Celebration Belle.
  2. For a shorter, more casual river tour, take a Channel Cat River Taxi and learn about the Mississippi River.
  3. There is also a path on both sides of the river that you can walk, run, or bike on. This is great for enjoying the river view while getting some exercise in. It is also the perfect place for pictures.

Rock Island

Rock Island Arsenal Museum

The Rock Island Arsenal is the largest weapon manufacturer in the country. Learn about this historical landmark in the Army's second oldest museum.

The Quad City Botanical Center

The Quad City Botanical Center, located by the Mississippi River, has a large variety of plants inside a 70-foot skylight garden. A perfect place for people of all ages.
