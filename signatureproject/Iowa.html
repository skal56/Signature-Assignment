Davenport

Downtown

Davenport's downtown area is a quaint city downtown lined with small businesses, colorful street art that gives the city a bright and lively atmosphere.

Figge

The Figge Art Meseum is a major art museum in Eastern Iowa and Western Illinois. that encorages community collaboration from Chicago to Des Moines through cultural exhibits and educational programs. The Figge is most famous for the horse sculpture named Half Moon.

Putnam

The Putnam Museam is an engaging and educational museum of history and natural science for people of all ages. The Putnam was one of the first meseums west of the Mississippi.

Bettendorf

Family Museum

The Family Museum is a great place for children to explore various areas from music to agriculture to math.

Splash Landing

The Splash Landing Aquatic Center is a pool open to everyone. This is the perfect place for children to cool down in the pool on a hot summer day.

BettPlex (TBK Bank Sports Complex)

The BettPlex is a relatively new family entertainment center that includes laser tag, bowling, escape rooms, indoor turf, basketball courts, outdoor baseball field, a food court, and much more.
